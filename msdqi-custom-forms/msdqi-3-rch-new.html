Instructions: Observe the service provider while attending a pregnant woman. At least two observations have to be conducted. If a facility has more than one service provider, observations should be made to different providers. If there is only one provider then all observations should be done for that health service provider. Do not interrupt unless the patient is severely ill or if the practice of the service provider will put the client in danger. If a patient is severely ill then the supervisor should assist in giving treatment while doing mentorship to the available clinicians and other health service providers.
