Section D. Dispensing Observations

Observations 1-3
Are you able to make an observation?
If No, Provide reason for not conducting the observation 

Instructions: Observe the dispenser while distributing treatments to patients with fever. If a facility has more than one dispenser observations should be made to different dispensers, if there is only one dispenser then all observations should be done on that dispenser. Do not interrupt unless the patient is severely ill or if the practice of the dispenser would put the patient in danger. If a patient is severely ill then the supervisor should help refer patient to relevant clinicians for care
