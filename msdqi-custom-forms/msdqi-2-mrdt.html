Section F. mRDT DQA: Sample Register Review

Instructions: Review the laboratory register for data completeness and consistency. Select the frst ten patients from the past month in the mRDT register. Select ‘Yes’ if the specifed information was recorded and ‘No’ if it was not. To calculate the patient identifcation score add up the number of `Yes’ answers in the following columns: Date, Name, Age, and Sex. To calculate the mRDT register score add up the number of `Yes’ answers in the following columns: Control line, PF line, Pan line, results recorded and correct interpretation. Correct Interpretation is determined by matching the Pf and Pan line results with the Results recorded. If both Pf and Pan lines are missing or Results are missing correct interpretations would be not determined (ND). The overall Register Review score is the average of these two sub-scores. Key: NR= Not recorded in register; Invalid = Invalid test result; ND = Not determined (due to missing Pf/Pan or result recorded).
